About This Game

Mastering flight, air-maneuvers and overcoming obstacles on unique levels awaits you in Space Rift. Many missions will take place in setting of deserts, barren ice planets, underground caves, megalopolises, inside giant space carriers and even deep under water! Missions objectives are anything from gathering of points to surviving in space with defective ship systems. Game allows for different ways of completing same level, which suits seasoned and new players. Also levels contain secrets and hidden mission objectives. Not to mention achievements and time trials.
